Users Personas

Users Research 

Before propelling on developing this RESTFUL Api, users research were carried out to understand their pain point. Various use case of different type of users were analyzed such as crypto trader, financial analyst to get a deep insight on their painpoint in the crypto currency financial world Below are the curated list of Users pain point and how they have been able to integrate the api in solving their challenge

Personas Block 

Cryptocurrency Trader

Erofede John

Pain Point: As a cryptocurrency trader, John needs real-time and accurate data on various coins to make informed trading decisions. It is crucial for him to have access to up-to-date coin prices, rankings, price changes, and contract addresses. Additionally, he requires the ability to download detailed coin data summaries for further analysis.

Scenario: As a cryptocurrency trader, John want to stay updated with the latest information about different coins, their prices, and market trends. He decides to find a reliable API endpoint that provides comprehensive data on coins, including their live prices, rankings, and price changes, inclusive with the ability to download this data for offline analysis.

Use Case: John uses the /coins endpoint to retrieve current live coin data. This endpoint provides him with insightful details about the coin's price, rank, price changes within a specific time period, and even its contract address if available. By regularly accessing this endpoint, John stays informed about the latest market trends, identify potential investment opportunities, and he is able to make a well-informed trading decisions.

Case Study: John is a cryptocurrency trader who actively trades different coins in the market. He relies on accurate and real-time data to identify potential trading opportunities and optimize his trading strategies. John integrates the cryptocurrency coin API into his trading platform and utilizes the /coins endpoint to fetch live coin data. By leveraging this endpoint, John can access the most up-to-date information about various coins, including their prices, rankings, and price changes. This helps him monitor market trends, identify coins with significant price movements, and make timely trading decisions. The ability to retrieve coin contract addresses also allows John to verify the authenticity of the coins he trades. Overall, the /coins endpoint provided by the API plays a vital role in John's trading activities, enabling him to stay competitive in the dynamic cryptocurrency market.

Scenario:As a trader, I want to compare paired prices of a specific coin across various exchanges to identify the most favorable trading opportunities. I require an API endpoint that can provide me with a comprehensive list of paired prices from all available exchanges.

This endpoint allows me to gather information on all available pairs and their corresponding prices. By analyzing this data, I can identify exchanges with the most favorable prices for a specific asset and execute trades accordingly. This helps me optimize my trading strategies and potentially capitalize on arbitrage opportunities.
